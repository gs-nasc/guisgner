[ ABOUT ] AFTER THE STORM
A brand is a living system — strategy, organization, narrative and creative direction.

Guilherme Costa is a Branding Designer and Creative Lead based in Santos, SP, working at the intersection of strategy, methodology and visual direction.

His trajectory is defined by a structural approach to design — beginning with the rational and creative construction of visual identities, exploring mathematical proportions and modular systems, and evolving into the strategic field of branding, where positioning, brand architecture and planning became central to his creative process.

Influenced by thinkers such as David Aaker, Philip Kotler, Ana Couto and Galileu Nogueira, he structures his work around strategic clarity, systemic thinking and consistent execution — building brands with longevity, coherence and real impact.
